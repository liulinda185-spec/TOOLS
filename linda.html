About Me

My journey towards becoming a leading sustainability expert contributing to global SDG achievement

Undergraduate student of Energy Science & Engineering at City University of Hong Kong (2023-2027), GPA 3.37/4.30, recipient of Hong Kong SAR TDS Scholarship, and HKIE Youth Energy Ambassador. My work focuses on low-carbon technology, Carbon Capture, Utilization and Storage (CCUS), green hydrogen energy, and AI applications for Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

With internship experience at CLP Power Hong Kong, Beijing Circular Economy Association and other renowned institutions, I have led and participated in multiple sustainable development research and entrepreneurship projects. My core mission is to drive global carbon neutrality through technological innovation and cross-industry collaboration to achieve UN SDGs.

My professional vision: To become a leading sustainability expert who bridges technology, policy, and business to accelerate the transition to a low-carbon future aligned with SDG targets.
